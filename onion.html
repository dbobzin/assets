Protection

We like our crops protected; we know you do too.

We specialize in spraying your fields with pesticides to protect your crops. The pesticides we use entirely depend on what pests you have, but we use Chlorpyriphos 20EC for a typical application. This chemical is a contact poison, and its composition is that of chlorine. Another pesticide we use is Acephate 70SP which is commonly used for onion flies and spider mites.
We recommend to spray for pests bi-monthly, but if you have a large issue you can spray up to once a month. Our primary method is crop dusting, but we can also use helicopters and in rare situations we burn fields. We also offer irrigation solutions, harvest / field inspection, and crop advice.
